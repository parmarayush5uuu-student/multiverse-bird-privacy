Privacy Policy for Multiverse Bird

Last updated: 12-12-2025

Multiverse Bird, developed by Ayush, is a casual mobile game that displays ads using Google AdMob. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The Game itself does not collect personal information. Third-party services (AdMob) may automatically collect device data, IP address, ad interaction data, approximate location, cookies, and diagnostic logs.

2. How We Use This Information

Information is used for showing ads, preventing fraud, improving performance, and analytics. We do not sell or share user data.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses Google AdMob. Learn more: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13.

5. Data Security

We do not store data ourselves. AdMob handles data securely using Google’s servers.

6. User Consent

AdMob may show consent forms depending on region (GDPR/CCPA).

This policy may be updated occasionally.
